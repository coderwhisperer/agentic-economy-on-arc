Why this works

Unit economics — the same $0.003 inference, three ways to settle it.

card processor
−$0.30
Stripe's minimum fee is $0.30 + 2.9%. A 3-cent inference is mathematically dead. You'd need a subscription wrapper, which is the opposite of per-interaction.
ethereum l1, per-call
−$0.20
Gas for one ERC-20 transfer dwarfs the entire revenue of the call. You lose 60× your price tag on gas alone, every time.
arc + circle nanopayments
+$0.0029
Gasless to the buyer, batched settlement absorbed at Circle's layer. The full price goes to the seller. This is the ONLY rail that makes the product exist.
